## ğŸ“ Design Decisions

### Why Two-Tier Project Management?

**Decision:** Adopt .STATUS + PROJECT-HUB.md system from zsh-configuration

**Rationale:**
- Prevents cognitive overload (don't think strategy when doing tasks)
- Enables quick context recovery (< 5 seconds)
- Separates tactical (today) from strategic (this week/month)
- ADHD-friendly: Right level of detail for right purpose

**Trade-offs:**
- âœ… Pro: Clear separation of concerns
- âœ… Pro: Faster daily updates
- âœ… Pro: Better strategic planning
- âš ï¸ Con: Two files to maintain (but different cadences)

---

### Why Not Build Software?

**Decision:** Keep Nexus as documentation/template, not installable software

**Rationale:**
- Users need to customize for their domain
- Obsidian already provides the platform
- Templates more flexible than rigid software
- Lower maintenance burden
- Easier to fork and adapt

**Trade-offs:**
- âœ… Pro: Infinitely customizable
- âœ… Pro: No dependency management
- âœ… Pro: Works with any Obsidian version
- âš ï¸ Con: Requires manual setup
- âš ï¸ Con: No "one-click install"

---

### Why PARA Method?

**Decision:** Use PARA (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives) for folder structure

**Rationale:**
- Industry-standard knowledge management framework
- Clear separation of active vs reference material
- ADHD-friendly (reduces decision fatigue)
- Scales well (works for 10 or 1000 notes)
- Well-documented methodology

**Trade-offs:**
- âœ… Pro: Familiar to PKM community
- âœ… Pro: Clear organizational logic
- âœ… Pro: Reduces "where does this go?" paralysis
- âš ï¸ Con: Some items could fit multiple categories
- âš ï¸ Con: Requires discipline to maintain
